The Company

The journey of R K Group began with a warehouse at Kishangarh, Rajasthan in 1989 and over the span of few years, diversified by expanding its geographical boundaries within the state in the form of villages Dharmeta, Morwad, Dhariyawad, Banswara in Rajasthan and village Majoli in Madhya Pradesh.Established in 1989 and headquartered in Kishangarh, Rajasthan, R K Marble Private Limited is a global leader in the natural stone industry, famously recognized by the Guinness World Records as the world's largest producer of marble. The company, led by Chairman Ashok Patni and Managing Director Vikas Patni, operates a massive supply chain that includes its own quarries in India and high-quality mines in Vietnam. Beyond its signature "Flawless White" marble, the company offers an extensive portfolio of over 500 stone varieties, including granite, onyx, and travertine, showcased in world-class galleries like Experience One in Gurugram. As the flagship entity of the R K Group, it maintains a strong synergy with its sister brands , Wonder Cement and Wonder Home Finance, while continuing to dominate the luxury architectural and construction markets through innovation and sustainable mining practices.Production and Reach: The company processes over 2 million cubic feet of raw marble annually. It has expanded its international footprint to over 45 countries, supported by wholly-owned mining operations in the Yên Bái Province of Vietnam, which spans 50 hectares and holds reserves of nearly 7.9 million cubic meters. Luxury Market Focus: In late 2025, R K Marble highlighted a shift in luxury interior trends toward colored and matte-finish marbles. New collections such as Infinity Gold—featuring gold veining on a midnight blue background—cater to this demand for unique, statement-making stones. Infrastructure: Its Experience One gallery in Gurugram and Mayapuri showroom serve as primary hubs for architects and designers to view large-scale, backlit, and custom-cut installations.
